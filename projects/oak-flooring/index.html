Elegant Oak Flooring

A premium oak flooring installation featuring custom staining and a classic herringbone pattern. This project showcases our attention to detail and commitment to quality craftsmanship.

Project Specifications

  • Material: Solid oak hardwood
  • Pattern: Herringbone
  • Finish: Custom stain with protective coating
  • Area: 1,200 sq ft
  • Duration: 2 weeks

Project Process

  1. Initial consultation and space measurement
  2. Subfloor preparation and leveling
  3. Pattern layout and planning
  4. Installation of oak planks in herringbone pattern
  5. Custom staining process
  6. Multiple layers of protective coating
  7. Final inspection and cleanup
