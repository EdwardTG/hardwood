Modern Maple Design

A contemporary maple flooring installation designed for open-concept spaces. This project showcases the natural beauty of maple wood while incorporating modern design elements for a clean, minimalist aesthetic.

Project Specifications

  • Material: Natural maple hardwood
  • Pattern: Linear planks with seamless transitions
  • Finish: Clear coat with satin sheen
  • Area: 1,500 sq ft
  • Duration: 2 weeks

Project Gallery

Open concept space with seamless maple flooring

Detail of the natural maple grain and clear finish

Precision installation ensuring perfect alignment

Seamless transition between living spaces

Project Process

  1. Space assessment and design planning
  2. Material selection and acclimation
  3. Removal of existing flooring
  4. Subfloor preparation and leveling
  5. Precision installation of maple planks
  6. Fine sanding and surface preparation
  7. Clear coat application
  8. Final buffing and quality check
