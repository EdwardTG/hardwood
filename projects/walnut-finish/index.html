Classic Walnut Finish

A sophisticated walnut flooring installation that combines traditional elegance with modern aesthetics. This project demonstrates our ability to blend classic materials with contemporary design preferences.

Project Specifications

  • Material: Engineered walnut
  • Pattern: Wide plank layout
  • Finish: Matte finish with natural oil treatment
  • Area: 950 sq ft
  • Duration: 10 days

Project Process

  1. Site evaluation and moisture testing
  2. Acclimation of walnut planks
  3. Subfloor preparation and leveling
  4. Installation of moisture barrier
  5. Wide plank installation
  6. Natural oil application
  7. Matte finish coating
  8. Final inspection and cleanup
